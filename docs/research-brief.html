Executive summary

Laв‚ѓNiв‚‚Oв‚‡ bilayer nickelate superconductivity was discovered under high pressure in July 2023 (Tc в‰€ 80K). Within 33 months, ambient-pressure superconductivity has been achieved in epitaxially strained thin films, with onset Tc rising from 42K (Dec 2024) to 63K (Mar 2026). Bulk records under pressure have reached 96K in Sm-substituted bilayer crystals. The gap between the ambient film record and the liquid nitrogen threshold (77K) is now only 14K.

This brief presents a structured literature synthesis of published Laв‚ѓNiв‚‚Oв‚‡ family measurements, with ambient thin films, films measured under applied pressure, and bulk-pressure anchors kept distinct. It identifies empirical patterns and research gaps, then lists five ranked, testable hypotheses for crossing the 77K threshold at ambient pressure. Those hypotheses are empirical extrapolations, not verified published measurements.

Key patterns extracted from cross-referencing all data

Pattern 1SLAO dominates high-Tc ambient films. Ambient LAO, NGO, and STO films are present in the curated dataset, but every ambient film above 40K uses SrLaAlOв‚„. No substrate with a < 3.75Г… has been tested (SmAlOв‚ѓ 3.734Г…, EuAlOв‚ѓ 3.720Г… remain untried).

Pattern 2Growth method is a leading variable in the current dataset. GAE extreme non-equilibrium growth produced 63K onset on the same substrate where PLD gives 42K. This is a large synthesis-associated effect in one same-substrate comparison and should be replicated before being treated as a general rule.

Pattern 3Oxygen vacancies occupy different sites in films vs. bulk. Compressive strain pushes vacancies from apical O (bulk) to planar NiOв‚‚ sites (films), changing which oxygen sites are critical for SC optimization (Nat. Mater. 2025).

Pattern 4Superconducting dome resembles cuprate phenomenology. Sr-doping maps a dome (x = 0 to 0.38) with a Hall sign change near optimal doping, echoing electron-doped cuprate phenomenology without proving the same mechanism (PRL 136, Feb 2026).

Pattern 5ОіII Fermi pocket is a reported SC-associated signature. Present in reported SC superstructures (1212, 2222, 2323), absent in non-SC 1313 вЂ” a candidate structural screening criterion that should be checked against the original figures before reuse (Nature, Apr 9 2026).

Pattern 6Strange-metal behavior tracks Tc in this small set. Highest-Tc films (63K) show resistivity в€ќ TВ№ (О± в‰€ 1), while lower-Tc films show Fermi-liquid TВІ dependence. The association is plausible but small-n.

Pattern 7c/a ratio is a leading empirical parameter within the measured series. In the curated substrate/pressure series, onset Tc increases with c/a (Comm. Phys. 2025). c/a encodes both strain and orbital splitting effects; extrapolation outside the measured c/a range is the risky step.

Research gaps вЂ” untested combinations

Gap 1No films on substrates with a < 3.75Г…. Stronger compression could push c/a higher and increase Tc вЂ” or cause film decoherence. This is the most obvious untested axis.

Gap 2Sr-doping dome only mapped on SLAO. Does optimal doping shift on different substrates? Zero data exists.

Gap 3Electric field gating of single-bilayer films proposed theoretically (Nat. Commun. Jan 2026, predicts 77K+). Zero experimental attempts published.

Gap 4Pressure applied to GAE-grown 63K films not yet reported. Lower-quality films already show 62в†’68.5K at 2 GPa (arXiv:2603.29531).

Gap 5No lanthanide sweep beyond Pr substitution. Nd, Sm, Gd on La site untested in films.

Gap 6Pairing symmetry in films unconfirmed experimentally вЂ” sВ± vs d-wave has direct implications for optimization strategy.

Ranked experiment prompts

These are empirical, hypothesis-generating experiment rankings derived from the curated measurements and cited trend claims. Labels describe heuristic priority/proximity, not probability. They are not first-principles simulations and are not verified outcomes.

P1 Near-term test
Apply 2вЂ“5 GPa pressure to GAE-grown 63K (La,Pr)в‚ѓNiв‚‚Oв‚‡ films.

Films in the cited pressure series report 62в†’68.5K at 2 GPa (Zhao/Sun 2026). If GAE films with fewer oxygen vacancies follow the same empirical pressure trend, a hypothesis-generating scenario range is 75вЂ“85K. Achievable with piston-cylinder pressure cells, not diamond anvils, but still unverified for the 63K GAE films.

P2 Extrapolative test
GAE-grown (La,Pr)в‚ѓNiв‚‚Oв‚‡ on SmAlOв‚ѓ (a = 3.734Г…, strain в‰€ -2.6%).

If the c/a trend holds beyond the measured range, extra compression could increase onset Tc by 10вЂ“15K. Key risk: coherent film growth at higher mismatch. No data exists for this substrate.

P3 Combination test
Optimal Sr-doping (x в‰€ 0.15) combined with GAE growth on SLAO.

The dome study used MBE, not GAE. Combining the best doping level with the best growth method has not been attempted. Directly targets the zero-R Tc gap.

P4 Theory-led test
Perpendicular electric field gating of single-bilayer films (0.1вЂ“0.2V).

Theory paper published in Nat. Commun. (2026): intralayer d-wave pairing enhanced to 77K+. Zero experimental attempts in the curated dataset. Any group with ionic liquid gating capability could test the claim.

P5 Exploratory
Engineer superstructures on substrates with a < 3.75Г….

The ОіII pocket is reported as an SC-associated signature (Nature, Apr 9 2026). Test whether stronger strain preserves or destroys this pocket while pushing Tc higher. Requires ARPES + film growth capability.

Rate of progress and projections

From first ambient film (42K, Dec 2024) to current record (63K, Mar 2026): +21K in 15 months. If that empirical rate continued unchanged, ambient films would cross 77K by mid-2027. This is a projection, not a guarantee; adoption of GAE and exploration of substrates, doping, and electric fields could change the slope in either direction.

The film + moderate pressure route is a near-term hypothesis: current films reach 68.5K at 2 GPa. Extrapolating the observed ~3K/GPa sensitivity, 5 GPa would land near ~77K. This is testable with clamp pressure cells, but it remains an unverified extrapolation for GAE-grown 63K films.

About this analysis

All 23 curated records trace to specific publications cited in the dataset. Hypothesis-candidate rows are empirical trend extrapolations, not first-principles simulations or forecasts, and should be treated as experimental follow-up prompts.
